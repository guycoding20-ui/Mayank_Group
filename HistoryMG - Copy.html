The Mayank Group Legacy: A History of Trust

2006: The Foundation

The journey began with a singular focus on **quality and integrity** in the Vasai-Virar region. Our founder established the group to address the community's need for reliable, high-standard housing. The first few projects set a benchmark for commitment and ethical business practices that continues today.

2008-2015: Rapid Growth & Area Mastery

This decade saw significant expansion across **Vasai East** and **Nalasopara**. We delivered numerous successful mid-size residential complexes, cementing our reputation as masters of local geography and compliant development. Our focus shifted to scalable models while retaining the personal touch of a community builder.

2023- Present: Modernization & Legacy Building

The group adapted to modern needs by pioneering complex **redevelopment projects** and embracing sustainable building technologies. Expansion into **Vasai West** and **Boisar** broadened our reach. We introduced the **'Imperial Lifestyle'** concept, focusing on providing enhanced amenities and superior living experiences.
