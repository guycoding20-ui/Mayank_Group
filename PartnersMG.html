Founding Owner

Manoj Kedia

Chairman & Visionary Leader

Manoj Kedia started the Mayank Group in 2006 with a commitment to quality, trust, and community upliftment. His four decades of experience in construction and financial ethics form the bedrock of the company's integrity and long-term vision. He guides the group's overall strategic direction and ethical mandate.

Under his visionary leadership, Mayank Group has transformed from a local builder to a trusted name in real estate development across Vasai, Nalasopara, and Boisar. Manoj Kedia's deep understanding of the local market, combined with his unwavering commitment to ethical business practices, has enabled the group to deliver numerous successful residential and commercial projects that have enriched thousands of families.

His philosophy centers on creating not just buildings, but communities that foster growth, prosperity, and lasting value. Through his guidance, Mayank Group has pioneered innovative redevelopment projects, modern residential complexes, and sustainable building practices that set new benchmarks in the industry. His hands-on approach and attention to detail ensure that every project reflects the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.

Beyond business success, Manoj Kedia is deeply committed to social responsibility and community development. He believes in giving back to the communities that have supported Mayank Group's growth, making the company not just a builder of homes, but a builder of dreams and futures.
